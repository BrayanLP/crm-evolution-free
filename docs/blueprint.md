# **App Name**: LeadFlow CRM

## Core Features:

- Lead Creation: Users can create new lead entries with essential information such as name, contact details, and company.
- Kanban Board View: Display leads as interactive cards organized into customizable stages (e.g., New, Contacted, Qualified, Converted) on a Kanban board.
- Drag-and-Drop Workflow: Enable users to update lead status effortlessly by dragging and dropping lead cards between different Kanban stages.
- Lead Detail Editor: Clicking on a lead card reveals a detailed view allowing for editing of comprehensive lead information and notes.
- AI Action Item Tool: An AI-powered tool that analyzes lead notes to suggest potential next steps or follow-up actions, enhancing sales efficiency.
- Data Persistence: Securely store and retrieve all lead data for continuous access and management.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated mid-tone blue (#2268DB) to convey professionalism and clarity, suitable for headings and interactive elements.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue-grey (#ECF0F7) to create a clean, calm, and focus-oriented canvas.
- Accent color: A vibrant cyan (#55C9DF) used sparingly for calls-to-action, status indicators, and highlighting key information.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective, and highly readable qualities, ideal for a data-intensive business application.
- Utilize clear and intuitive iconography with a contemporary, outlined style for actions such as adding new leads, editing details, and indicating lead progression.
- Employ a responsive Kanban board layout with distinct, well-defined columns for stages, and clean, card-based UI elements for individual leads, optimized for usability across devices.
- Implement smooth, subtle drag-and-drop animations for lead cards as they transition between stages, providing immediate visual feedback to the user.
